DESCRIPTION:
A collection of 40+ stylized low poly weapons 
and shields perfect for any fantasy RPG or 
action game. Each asset is optimized for 
performance and ready to use in your project.

================================================
CONTENTS:
================================================

WEAPONS (30+):
- Swords (multiple tiers)
- Axes and War Axes
- Spears and Pikes
- Hammers and Mauls
- Scythes and Hooks
- Daggers and Blades

SHIELDS (10+):
- Round Shields
- Tower Shields
- Kite Shields
- Sigil Shields

================================================
WEAPON TIERS:
================================================

Novice     → starter weapons
Apprentice → early game weapons
Expert     → mid game weapons
Master     → late game weapons
Legendary  → The King Of Demon's 👑

================================================
TECHNICAL INFO:
================================================

- Polygon count: 80 to 250 faces per model
- Texture: Single 8x8 Atlas Texture
- Format: FBX
- Unity version: 6.0 and above
- Render Pipeline: Built-in / URP / HDRP

================================================
SETUP:
================================================

1. Import package into your Unity project
2. Navigate to Prefabs folder
3. Drag any prefab into your scene
4. Open Demo Scene to see full showcase
5. Customize materials as needed
